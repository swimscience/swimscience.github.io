Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 14, 2025

Introduction

SwimScience ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our swimming calculator tools and website.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use our calculators, you voluntarily enter swimming data such as:

  • Race times and distances
  • Stroke counts
  • Pool lengths
  • Training zone preferences

This information is processed locally in your browser and is not stored on our servers.

Automatically Collected Information

We use analytics services to understand how visitors use our site:

  • Google Analytics 4: Tracks page views, calculator usage, and general site interaction
  • Microsoft Clarity: Records user sessions to help us improve website usability

This data includes: browser type, device type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and general geographic location (country/city level).

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Analytics and performance measurement
  • Remembering your calculator preferences (stored locally)
  • Affiliate link tracking (required for Amazon Associates program)

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our calculator tools
  • Understand which features are most valuable to swimmers
  • Fix bugs and optimize performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Analytics Providers: Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity process usage data under their own privacy policies
  • Affiliate Programs: Amazon Associates receives anonymous click data when you use affiliate links
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law

Data Security

All calculator data is processed client-side (in your browser). We do not store your swimming times, distances, or personal calculations on our servers. Analytics data is protected according to the respective service providers' security standards.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses these third-party services:

  • Google Analytics 4: Privacy Policy
  • Microsoft Clarity: Privacy Policy
  • Amazon Associates: Privacy Notice

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request what data we have about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object: Object to data processing
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data collection

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@swimscience.org

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when changes were made. Continued use of our site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
