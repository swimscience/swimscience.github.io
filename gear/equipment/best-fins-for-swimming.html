Buying Guide

Short-blade fins are best for technique and cadence; longer fins build pure strength but can alter stroke mechanics. Choose a snug fit without pinching.

FAQs

What size should I pick?

Follow brand size charts; consider neoprene socks if between sizes.

Short vs long blade?

Short for high-cadence technique, long for leg strength blocks.
